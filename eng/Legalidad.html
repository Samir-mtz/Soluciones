FAQ

What is an assignment of litigious rights?+

In Mexico, it is the acquisition of a right over an overdue mortgage loan, transmitted by a banking institution, guaranteed by a property, which is acquired at the end of the judicial process.

What is an adjudication?+

In Mexico, it is a domain right over a property acquired at the end of a judicial process, endorsed by aa judge and a public notary.
